Your Personal Health Companion

Take control of your wellbeing. InstaDoc makes it simple to track your vitals, understand your health trends, and connect with your doctor anytime, anywhere.

Whether you are managing a chronic condition, monitoring post-procedure recovery, or simply staying on top of your health, InstaDoc gives you the tools to understand your body and collaborate meaningfully with your care team.

Vitals & Metrics Logging

Log blood pressure, weight, glucose levels, and BMI with ease. Track trends over time with intuitive charts and receive AI-generated summaries of your health trajectory.

Personalised AI Health Insights

InstaDoc's AI analyses your latest readings using clinically validated guidelines (AHA/ACC) and delivers plain-language feedback, helping you understand what your numbers mean.

Easy Appointment Booking

Book consultations directly with your doctor from within the app. Choose between in-person and virtual (video/audio) visits, and receive automatic reminders before your appointment.

Health History & Reports

Access your complete longitudinal health record. Export a PDF health report to share with any healthcare provider, perfect for specialist referrals and second opinions.

Smart Reminders

Set daily reminders to log your measurements so you never miss a reading. Consistent tracking is the foundation of meaningful health insights.

Private & Secure

Your health data belongs to you. InstaDoc uses bank-grade encryption and is fully HIPAA compliant. You control what is shared and with whom, always.
