Solutions for Healthcare Providers

Equip your clinical teams with intelligent tools for patient monitoring, workflow efficiency, and data-driven care decisions, all in one secure platform.

From individual practitioners to multi-specialty clinics, InstaDoc gives healthcare providers the real-time visibility and analytical depth to deliver proactive, personalised care while reducing administrative burden.

Patient Vitals Monitoring

Track blood pressure, weight, glucose, and other metrics for your entire patient panel in real time. Receive automated alerts when readings fall outside clinically defined thresholds.

Appointment & Schedule Management

Manage your appointment calendar with intelligent conflict detection, automatic patient reminders, and telemedicine (video/audio) integration, reducing no-shows by up to 40%.

Clinical Notes & Patient Records

Document consultations, treatment plans, and follow-up instructions within a HIPAA-compliant record system. Access full patient health history at a glance during every appointment.

AI-Powered Clinical Insights

Leverage AHA/ACC-aligned AI analysis to surface early warning indicators for hypertension, glucose dysregulation, and other chronic conditions, supporting timely clinical intervention.

Performance Dashboards

Visualise your practice metrics: consultation volumes, patient outcomes, appointment completion rates, and more. Use data to continuously improve your care delivery model.

Secure Provider Portal

A dedicated, role-based interface ensures doctors only see their own patients' data. Full audit logging, two-factor authentication, and end-to-end encryption are standard.
