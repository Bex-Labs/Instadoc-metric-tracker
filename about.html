About InstaDoc Metric Tracker

Pioneering healthcare analytics excellence in Nigeria and beyond

Our Story

InstaDoc Metric Tracker was founded with a singular mission: to transform healthcare delivery in Nigeria through innovative data analytics and technology. We recognized that healthcare providers were making critical decisions without access to comprehensive, real-time data, leading to inefficiencies and suboptimal patient outcomes.

Today, we are proud to be Nigeria's leading healthcare analytics platform, serving over 50 healthcare facilities across the country. Our team of healthcare professionals, data scientists, and technology experts work tirelessly to deliver solutions that make a real difference in patient care.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the complex world of health data. By tracking every vital sign with precision, we provide patients and providers with the clarity needed to make life-enhancing decisions together.

Our Vision

To become the universal language of health metrics, where every individual has a clear window into their well-being and every healthcare provider has the real-time insights to deliver personalized, proactive care.
